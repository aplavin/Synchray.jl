Synchray.jl — Conceptual Overview

This page is meant as a conceptual overview (not API docs).

Magnetic field

  • BFieldSpec(scale, dir, wrap)
  • scale: Profiles.* (e.g. Profiles.Axial(PowerLaw(...))) for spatial dependence
  • dir: Directions.Axial/Radial/Toroidal/Helical(ψ)
  • wrap: ordered / FullyTangled / TangledOrderedMixture

Electrons

  • density: Profiles.* (e.g. Profiles.Axial(PowerLaw(...)))
  • Isotropic- or Anisotropic- PowerLawElectrons

Microphysics

given local state (nₑ, B′) and k′, compute coefficients

outputs: comoving emissivity/absorption jν′, αν′ (including polarization)

Geometries

  • ray intersection z_interval(), containment is_inside()
  • Geometries.Conical(axis, φj, z) with natural coords (z, ρ, η)
  • slab / sphere / ellipsoid

Velocity

  • VelocitySpec(dir, kind, scale)
  • dir: Directions.Axial/Radial/...
  • scale: Profiles.* (e.g. Profiles.Transverse(...)) for spatial dependence

Patterns/knots

  • Profiles.Modified(base, pattern) for particle density or another quantity
  • Patterns.EllipsoidalKnot(x_c0, u, sizing, profile)
  • SR-consistent world-tube, time-dependent modulation

EmissionRegion

geometry + ne + B + velocity + model

EmissionRegion <: AbstractSynchrotronMedium

a composed object the renderer queries along rays

Targets

  • Intensity(): I or IntensityIQU()
  • OpticalDepth() τ
  • SpectralIndex() α (using autodiff)

Camera

CameraZ(xys, nz, ν, t) or individual RayZ()

Units

  • ustrip(obj): convert unitful inputs
  • withunits(...): restore units in result

Render

render([ray or camera], medium, target)

integrate SR invariants along rays
